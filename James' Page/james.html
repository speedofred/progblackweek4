Positive Impacts of Computers: Computers in Education

Created by James Macdermott. Highlighting the factors making computers in education so important

Access To Education and Infomation using Computers

⚪Internet Access

Computers are the primary means of internet access. Internet access has allowed the sharing of knowledge on top of vast repositories of infomation and resources such as courses, journals and libraries.

⚪Online Courses

Ability to gain qualifications and knowledge, usually obtained within a physical place of education such as a school, now obtainable from your home.

⚪E-books and E-readers

Physical, and perhaps expensive or exclusive, books can now be accessed using the internet and there is less need for carrying many books when several can be accessed by even small computers such as phones. Devices such as phone also allow for the same portability of education and infomation that a book would hold.

Computers in Online Education

⚪Accessability

Computers allow for online education. It allows for anyone, provided they have a computer, to join online classes, read online class notes and much more. It breaks down the disadvantages of regular classes such as physical precense, and it allows for students from all over the world can access materials and courses anyone could.

⚪Convenience and Pacing

When it comes to materials such as online courses, students can use computers to access them whenever. This allows for more convenience as students can learn at their own pace, and at times they prefer or see fit.

⚪Lockdown

Computers facilitated an average of 50% of learning ability over the Covid-19 pandemic, possibly higher given avergae will vary amongst regions. Hence, computers have been a leading factor in helping those studying at GCSE to Degree level ensure they can keep up to a similar standard as they would if they were to attend physical lessons.

⚪Enhanced Creativity

Computers provide creation software, wether thats IDEs for programming, photoshop for images or other art softwares, it provides users with the means to enhance their skills and education by giving them ranges of tools to complete a task exactly how they would want to.

⚪Enhanced Education Tools

Durham's blackboard ultra and other tools such as gradescope, allow for majorities of education to be digitalised. This is important as lecturers have hundereds of students, hence it is more practical and useful to be able to communicate online, where students view questions and submit answers with the use of computers.

⚪Collaboration

Tools such as Github allow several users to work as a team when it comes to educational projects. This is because computers allow digital work to be sent and shared to servers, hence user's can view and edit other group members work online rather than meeting up in person, hence computers allow for further practicallity on top of the sharing of important infomation and education through software and application.
